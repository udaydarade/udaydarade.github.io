Some of the Novels I Have Read

The Alchemist

This is my favorite novel. It tells a magical story of a boy who goes on a journey to follow his dreams.

One of Us Is Lying

A mystery novel about high school students entangled in a murder investigation.

The Silent Patient

An intriguing psychological thriller about a woman who stops speaking after a shocking act.

Ikigai

A wonderful book that explores the Japanese secret to a long and happy life.

The Tale of Two Cities

A classic novel by Charles Dickens that portrays love and sacrifice during the French Revolution.

Foundation

A sci-fi masterpiece by Isaac Asimov about the rise and fall of civilizations in a futuristic universe.

Dune

An epic tale by Frank Herbert that blends politics, religion, and ecology in a futuristic desert world.

All the Light We Cannot See

A beautifully written WWII-era novel by Anthony Doerr about a blind French girl and a German boy whose paths collide.

The Kite Runner

A heart-touching story about friendship and redemption set in Afghanistan.

The King's General

A historical romance by Daphne du Maurier with a gripping tale set in the English Civil War.

And Then There Were None

An Agatha Christie masterpiece about ten strangers trapped on an island with a dark secret.

The Girl on the Train

A suspenseful novel about a woman who becomes entangled in a missing person's case.
